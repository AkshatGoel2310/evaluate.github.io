About

EVALU is a personalized test series and analysis program which helps high school students prepare for their final school examinations through a personalized progress report highlighting their areas of strengths and weaknesses.

We believe it doing the right way and therefore guide you through the right part in your academic journey. Currently we are commencing with our program for Class 12 for Accountancy subject. Our program runs a monthly subscription. Our subscriptions are divided in two categories – Analysis Based Program and Test-series Based Program.

These tests and reports are designed to make it extremely comprehendible and user-friendly. Our aim is not only to help the students with their struggles to solve the Accountancy tests in time but also provide them with practices of writing the examinations in one setting and develop the required mindset for their Board Examinations.
